C Mart - Virtual Grocery Shop

2a. For one of your competitors, write a brief description of their business as contrasted to yours

One of our major competitors is Rosie App. Rosie is an on-line shopping store, enabling its customers to shop for groceries from a variety of local stores. Rosie offers its customers in-store pickup and door delivery. It charges a convenience fee for each order and requires that the order be of a minimum amount before checkout.

CMart is different from Rosie in a number of ways, shown below:

  CMart Rosie
Revenue Model CMart offers free delivery for orders above a minimum value. It charges a small but affordable fee for small orders. In addition, a very small order processing fee is charged for each order. Rosie charges its users a convenience and delivery fee for all orders. It even charges a fuel surcharge for door delivery
Social and Health Features CMart allows its customers to know what their friends are buying, receive recommendations and read review by other CMart customers. It also advises on dietary requirements. Customers can track their usage, spends and nutritional information of their orders. CMart encourages healthy eating and living with convenience of getting healthy food at their fingertips. Rosie predicts the household items you need before you run out, and enables you to order them at the lowest price from local grocers and online retailers.
Loyalty and Rewards CMart will offer rewards irrespective of the store you buy from and you can redeem those credits at any of CMart's affiliated stores Rosie doesn't have a full fledged loyalty system yet.

2b. describe what your competitive advantage might be

The following are CMart's competitive advantages:

  1. CMart is the most cost-effective solution available in the market. It enables its cusomters to see a variety of deals from multiple partners and choose the lowest price with the best quality.
  2. CMart is proudly mobile first. At CMart, we have realised that the future lies in mobile devices. CMart launched with an ios app for apple devices and has a strong focus on developing the platform with a mobile first approach. We want to enable our customers to order grocery from anywhere and everywhere with an intuitive interface they will love.
  3. CMart has a strong social focus too. Customers are much more willing to buy a product if their friends recommend it. Also, knowing about how other users found a product helps customers make the right choice while shopping.

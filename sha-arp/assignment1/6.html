6a. Explain how online education can be a disruptive technology in education industry. What are the advantages of online education? What are the disadvantages?

Online education has offered doors to higher education to students who lack access to elite universities. It has even lead to high paying jobs and sophisticated skills without tuition or a college degree. Some of these courses also come with informal credential of course completion. All these accounts as a threat to traditional education and hence proves to be disruptive technology in education industry. Availability, access, low cost, benefits as explained above along with encouragement to understand the subject matter and gain higher grades, social interaction through discussion boards are some of the advantages of online education. Some of the disadvantages could be - it might lead to issues in copyrights, giving away official credentials of courses, students need to be highly motivated to complete the course

6b .Explain why disruptive technologies are often low quality initially. Why is low quality an advantage? How do they overcome the quality issue?

Disruptive technologies are often low quality to keep the product simple and cost of the product at a much lower price than its superior counterpart. This is an advantage as for many consumer simple product with lower price is an obvious choice than a complex expensive product.

Over the time these product improves on its quality by the virtue that product improve more rapidly than consumer needs, and hence sooner performance becomes good enough . Adding additional complexity to increase performance is actively harmful to customer’s user experience. Disruptive technology strikes a right balance with simplicity and performance.

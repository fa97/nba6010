We created CMart's database and added Customer table to the database.

Customer(email, customer_name, address, phone, state)

Our table consists of 5 columns to capture the customer details.

  1. The email field stores the email of the customer
  2. Customer_name field stores the customer name
  3. address stores the residential address of the customer
  4. phone is a ten digit field storing the phone number
  5. state is a two character field storing the two letter code of the customer state
